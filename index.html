About Me

I was born and raised in Pittsburgh. I attended Ithaca College where I majored in Architectural Studies with minors in music and art. I now live and work in Philadelphia where I will pursue my master's degree in Landscape Architecture. I hope to bring my programming knowledge into a design focus through parametrics and GIS data integration.


In my free time, I enjoy traveling, gardening, playing the saxophone, and reading.
